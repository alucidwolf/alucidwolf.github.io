History

Some more info on where I have been

  • 2010

    In The Beginning

    During my last year in college, I clearly realized that my love for working in the web would indeed prove to be a solid career choice. So I set out for my first actual job apart from the miniscule side projects. This was the beginning!

  • March 2011

    The Intern Shuffle

    Internships really helped me hone in on what I wanted to do as a career. Through some trial and error, and much experience learned, I was recruited to begin my trip down web developer lane with Cendyn.

  • September 2015

    Getting back on track

    During my tenure at Cendyn I rapidly climbed the ladder from the website builder position to manager of the entire Production team. After a year or so I struggled with the fact that I was not writing code as much as I would like.

  • March 2016

    Back on track

    The decision was made and an opportunity opened up for me to move over to the front end development team, and it was a great decision for the future of my career. I am much more hands on now, coding all day, and exploring new technologies which is such a thrill. Never stop learning!

  • November 2019

    Expanding the toolkit

    After a few years at Cendyn, an opportunity was brought to me to enter the Salesforce ecosystem and build beautiful, performant and accessible communities that bring employers, employees and clients together. I worked with a great team, and continued to hone my skills resulting in a promotion to Senior Front End Engineer.

  • April 2022

    Transition from Salesforce

    After 2.5 years working in the Salesforce ecosystem, I decided to make a move into a more modern tech stack by joining the Penn Interactive team to work on the Barstool and theScore Sportsbooks. They use Vue, React, TypeScript and many more technologies that I felt would continue to round out my skillset. It has been one of the most rewarding experiences I have had in my professional career.
